Data Dictionary

Thank you for submitting your request to us. At Grindr, your privacy is important to us and we strive to support your ability to access and understand the data we collect about you.

The information you will receive through our Download My Data tool is largely already available to you through the app and may vary, depending on the way you have used Grindr. In general, this information includes content and photos you have provided us, whether directly or through your social media accounts, as well as messages you have sent and other data you would expect to see from a social media service, like Grindr.

Below is a data dictionary that we are providing for your convenience, so you can understand the data returned to you, regardless of your technical knowledge and background. The data dictionary is a list of data file types contained within your data download and a description of the information included in those files. If a field is blank in your file, it means that we do not have that data for you and nulls are expected for some fields. The data shown below is stored by your mobile device, not by Grindr's servers. Also, the type of data you’ll receive can depend on a few factors. For example, if you recently uninstalled the app or deactivated your account, some data won’t be included as it’s no longer stored on your device. Timestamps are in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) unless otherwise indicated.

For additional information on how we use and share data, please refer to our Privacy Policy, or copy and paste this link: https://www.grindr.com/privacy-policy/.

Conversations

Data Category Data Element Definition
conversations (chat) title The display name(s) of the account holder that you sent chat message(s) on Grindr.
conversations (chat) message The chat messages you sent to other users on Grindr. The messages can include other media beyond text, such as images, audio files, gaymojis, location, taps, and links.
conversations (chat) type The type of chat you sent to other users on Grindr (e.g. text, images, audio files, giphys, gaymojis, location, taps, and links).
conversations (chat) content The contents of chat you sent to other users on Grindr.
conversations (chat) mediaUrl The URL of the media that you sent in chat to other users on Grindr.
conversations (chat) at The date and time of the chat that you sent to other users on Grindr.
conversations (chat) lat The latitude that you sent in chat when you shared your live location with other Grindr users.
conversations (chat) lon The longitude that you sent in chat when you shared your live location with other Grindr users.
conversations (group chat) title The name of the group chats in which you sent chat message(s) on Grindr.
conversations (group chat) chatType The chat type (e.g. "group") in which you sent chat messages on Grindr.
conversations (group chat) timestamp The timestamps in which you sent chat messages in a group chat on Grindr.
conversations (group chat) message The chat messages you sent in a group chat on Grindr. The messages can include other media beyond text, such as images, audio files, gaymojis, location, taps, and links.

Profile Notes

Data Category Data Element Definition
profile_notes name The display name(s) where you have a profile note. A profile note is your personal note that you added to a profile in your "Favorites" list.
profile_notes note The text of a profile note that you wrote.
profile_notes phoneNumber The phone number associated with a Grindr user where you supplied the number yourself as a profile note. This data element is only relevant to Android devices.
